Rajasthan

Regions of Rajasthan

Rajputana, as Rajasthan was previously known was subdivided into several regional units for ease of administration. Approaching the Jajam research on these regional basis brings out the uniqueness of Jajam in colors, motifs, nuances and themes with respect to each region.

Ajmer

Jotayan, Shreenagar, Pushkar, Akoria

Dhundhar

Jahota, Jairampura, Jaipur, Bagru

Marwar

Barmer, Balotra, Samdari, Jodhpur

Hadoti

Kota, Bundi, Jhalawar, Baran

Mewar

Udaipur, Rajsamand, Bhilwara, Chittorgarh

Shekhawati

Jhunjhunu, Sikar, Churu
